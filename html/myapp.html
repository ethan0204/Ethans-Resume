How to pick a paint scheme for my favorite army

There are many armies in the game of warhammer, but this one is my favorite!They are called the Adeptus Mechanicus, and here are some of the official paint schemes for the army. Have fun learning and picking!

I also attached a short piece of lore for each forge world, just to give an overview of what they are all about, but feel free to do whatever you like with each paint scheme.
